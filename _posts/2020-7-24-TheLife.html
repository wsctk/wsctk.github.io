What's life

Unexpected understanding

Life is a cup of coffee, what we do everyday is try to add different condiment to our cup in front the table to make the cofffee taste not so painful but other flavour we like or we want to like. Someone want it sweet, some other one maybe want it mellow. What we all should know is that the final taste of the it id absolutely decided by ourselves not any other one. Someone listens to other one so easily and at the same time has an unexpected taste of it, then they may think the coffee is designed to be so painful that people should bear it if they want to have water, so they refuse to try to turn it to some flavour they like. Everyone has an only cup of coffee, what you should do is to create your flavour, all of it are technically meaningless, but we choose to do it because that's what we who have no rights to choosewhen and where to born can do to feel whether we exist in this big, cold, complex universe!
